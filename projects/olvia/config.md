## What They Do
OLVIA is a vertically integrated oil extraction complex in southern Ukraine. They process oilseed crops (sunflower, soy, rapeseed) into crude oils, protein meal products for livestock feed, and biofuel pellets from husks.

**Products:**
- **Crude oils**: Sunflower, soy, and rapeseed (raw/unrefined)
- **Meal products**: Sunflower meal (35–38% protein), soy meal (44–46% protein) for livestock
- **Biofuel**: Pelleted sunflower husks for industrial heating

**Capacity:**
- Processing: 1,200 tons/day
- Annual production: 400,000 tons
- Storage: 84,700 m³ elevator capacity
- Two-month buffer inventory for contract reliability

**Logistics:**
- 178 km from Odesa Port — direct Black Sea export access
- Exports to 50+ countries worldwide
- On-site crop drying, cleaning, and quality enhancement services
