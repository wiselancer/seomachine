## What It Does
StackPass bundles 80+ premium marketing and SEO tools into a single $49/month subscription with pre-authenticated browser sessions. Users log in once and get instant access to all tools without creating individual accounts.

**Tool categories included:**
- Ad intelligence: AdSpy, Pipiads PRO
- AI platforms: ChatGPT Plus, Perplexity Pro
- Creative production: Leonardo AI, Sora, PicLumen
- SEO (65 tools): Ahrefs, Semrush, Majestic, SearchAtlas, Screaming Frog
- Campaign playbooks: e-commerce, SaaS, lead generation
- Multi-GEO coverage

**Access model**: Browser-based pre-authenticated sessions. One active user per subscription. No individual tool accounts needed.
