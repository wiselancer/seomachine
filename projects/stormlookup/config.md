## What It Does
Storm Lookup is a property storm damage assessment system that:
- Notifies property owners about severe weather events that may have damaged their property
- Provides free storm assessments to help with insurance claims
- Helps homeowners, insurance adjusters, and contractors assess hail/wind/storm damage
