# FruitfulCode AI Citation Targets

## Goal

Increase the chances that ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini cite Fruitful Code when users ask about software agency selection, MVP delivery, outsourcing, and real estate software development.

- agency vs freelancer for startup product development
- how to hire a mobile app development company
- questions to ask before hiring a software agency

### Startup Delivery
- how much an MVP costs
- how long it takes to build an MVP
- what founders should prepare before hiring a dev team
- common MVP mistakes for startups

### Outsourcing and Geography
- nearshore vs offshore development
- software development in Ukraine
- Eastern European development teams
- staff augmentation vs agency delivery

### Vertical Expertise
- how to build a real estate app
- real estate software development company
- custom property platform development
